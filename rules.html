Simple Team Rules

Short rules for a private Eteris team dashboard. This is a practical agreement between friends and teammates, not a legal document.

Version 2026-06-11-v8
Acceptance

Using the site means accepting these rules.

When this page changes, everyone will be asked to accept the new version again.

Be clear

Set your Premier status early enough so the team can plan without guessing.

Keep it useful

Use the dashboard and status tools for team organization. Do not spam or intentionally mislead people.

Respect edits

Admins can update score, map history, player status resets and charity penalties to keep the board accurate.

Status deadline

If you do not set Ready or Not ready by the day before Premier, you accept a 25 PLN or higher charity penalty.

Account data

The site stores account name, login code, display name, avatar, status and team dashboard data needed for the app.

Privacy

Do not store private passwords, personal documents or sensitive information here. Admins may access data while maintaining the project.

Fair play

Do not use the site for cheating, harassment, impersonation, doxxing or anything that breaks Riot Games rules.

Images

Uploaded avatars use Supabase storage. When an avatar is replaced or removed, the site attempts to clean up unused files.
